The Essence... of Presence

Presence depends on two things, visitors finding you and visitors remembering you. We help visitors find your site in a number of ways. The main source of new visitors is search engines, therefore being listed high in search results is important for a growing presence. By using a strategy called search engine optimization (SEO) I will put your site at the head of visitors searches. After those visitors have found you they need to remember you. They will take away one thing after leaving your site, an impression. That impression will include two things, your name and why you're awesome. With those two things visitors will be returning to your business at one point or another.
